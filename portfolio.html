This customer is vegan, and wanted a design which revolved around a tattoo which they had of a cartoon carrot!

I took their cartoon tattoo and created lots of different fruits and vegetables, all in different positions until we were happy with the layout.

Once we'd decided on the layout, I added them to the shoes along with a couple of personal touches, such as the crown, as David is also a huge fan of Strava KOMS!

Because he is such a fan of climbing, I also added some little abstract mountains on the inside of the shoe, just to add a little subtle contrast to the cartoons.
